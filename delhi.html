Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India. It is famous for Red Fort, India Gate, and street food at Chandni Chowk.


Delhi, the capital of India, is a vibrant city that beautifully blends history, culture, and modernity. With its rich heritage sites like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb, Delhi offers a glimpse into India's glorious past. At the same time, the city is a hub of innovation, commerce, and contemporary lifestyle, featuring modern infrastructure, shopping malls, and a dynamic tech industry. From the narrow lanes of Old Delhi filled with traditional markets and street food to the wide avenues of New Delhi with its government buildings and green spaces, the city showcases a unique mix of old and new. Known for its diversity, Delhi welcomes people from all over the country, making it a true cultural melting pot.
