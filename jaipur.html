Jaipur

Jaipur is called the Pink City. It is famous for Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and delicious Rajasthani food.


Jaipur, known as the “Pink City,” is the capital of Rajasthan and famous for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. The city is renowned for its majestic forts and palaces, such as the Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal, which showcase the grandeur of Rajputana heritage. Jaipur's colorful bazaars offer traditional crafts, textiles, and jewelry, making it a shopper's paradise. The city beautifully combines its royal past with modern amenities, attracting tourists from around the world. With its warm hospitality, festive spirit, and beautiful landscapes, Jaipur is a captivating destination that reflects the true essence of Rajasthan.
